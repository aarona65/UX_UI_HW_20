What's For Dinner?

Allowing busy individuals to easily scan food items to create curated recipes.

The Problem:

With a busy lifestyle, planning dinner can be challenging and time consuming, especially when you have more than yourself to consider.

The Solution:

An app that allows you to input and store your ingredients and curates recipes based on your preferences and needs.

The Goals:

The goal was to find a way to make cooking as seamless and customized as possible. Many individuals, are pressed for time and rarely find time to cook their meals. Finding a way to lessen that burden is imperative.

Definition & Ideation

User Insight:

Many home cooks have competing priorities. From their day jobs, to their hobbies or kids, they often find themselves challenged to cook healthy meals that cater to their or their families needs/specifications. From our user research data (interviews and survey), we found that many users feel stressed when planning and prepping meals that cater to all parties. I believe users would benefit from an app that delivers curated recipes based off of items on-hand and customizable preferences.

Problem Statement:

Our App was designed to allow home cooks to save time and money by alleviating the stresses that come with planning and cooking meals. Based on our user research, we have observed that many users struggle to balance their day-to-day responsibilities and finding the time to prepare meals specified for their/their family needs. We have also observed that while there are many apps that let you find recipes, there aren’t many that curate recipes based on items on-hand, as well as the specified needs. How might we create an app that allows home cooks to input items and customized preferences, and deliver curated, healthy meals?
